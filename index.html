Australian Football League

Mobile UI

Brief

UI/UX for a mobile app.

This is a mobile phone application for iPhone, Android, and Windows Mobile. Target handset and screens are iPhone 6 and 6+, Samsung Galaxy Note 4, S5, S4, Nokia Lumina 930, 635 The designs should be flat without specifics to iOS, Android, or Windows. The design will support multiple clubs with different logos and colors. There needs to be a general color scheme for the demonstration of the application

Overview

The Australian Football League (AFL) is the highest-level professional competition in the sport of Australian rules football.

This is a mobile application to help sports clubs engage with their members and for members to find local retailers that support their clubs.

Consumers will get the app to keep up to date on their clubs players, schedule of games, events, and news. Consumers will also be able to view a list of local retailers that offer incentives to the consumer and benefits for the club. For example a consumer can shop at the local sports store and get 10% cash back. The consumer gives 5% of that cash to the sports club and keeps 5% for themselves. The consumer decides what % goes to the club and what goes to themselves. The application allows the consumer to view their purchase history, savings, and how much they have donated to the club needs.

Solution

As the UI Designer my responsibility was of creating simple and elegant user experience designs and designing the entire flow of based fulfilling the requirements.
